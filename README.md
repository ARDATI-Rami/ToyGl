# ToyGl: Computational Biomechanics Simulation Framework

**Overview:**

ToyGl is an innovative Python-based code project meticulously crafted for the simulation of cellular behavior within a dynamic 3D environment. This sophisticated simulation framework draws inspiration from biomechanics, with a particular focus on unraveling the intricate dynamics governing epithelial tissue.

**Designed by Expertise:**

Developed by a dedicated Ph.D. student specializing in biomechanics, ToyGl showcases a synthesis of advanced computational models to meticulously simulate the nuanced interactions and behaviors exhibited by individual cells within a tissue context.

**Key Features:**

- **Realistic Cellular Dynamics:** ToyGl excels in modeling realistic cellular movements, interactions, and responses to environmental stimuli within the immersive landscape of three-dimensional space.

- **Biomechanical Principles:** Rooted in the principles of epithelial tissue biomechanics, ToyGl enables researchers and scientists to delve into the complex dynamics of cell behavior within tissues, providing a valuable lens for understanding physiological phenomena.

**Precision and Accuracy:**

Given its origins as a creation of a Ph.D. student, ToyGl inherently embodies a high level of precision and accuracy in its simulation algorithms. This precision ensures that researchers in the biomechanics field can rely on ToyGl for insightful exploration and analysis of the biomechanical properties of epithelial tissues under diverse conditions.

**Comprehensive Functionality:**

- **3D Visualization:** The codebase of ToyGl may encompass functionalities for visually representing the simulated 3D environment, enhancing the interpretability of simulation outcomes.

- **Cell Movement Tracking:** Researchers can leverage ToyGl to track cellular movements, providing a detailed understanding of how cells traverse and interact within the simulated environment.

- **Simulation Analysis:** The framework facilitates the analysis of simulation outcomes, empowering researchers to draw meaningful conclusions from the intricate data generated.

- **User-Friendly Interface:** ToyGl may offer a user-friendly interface, simplifying parameter adjustments and making it a versatile tool accessible to researchers exploring the biomechanics of epithelial tissues.

**Advancing Biomechanical Understanding:**

In the realm of computational biomechanics, ToyGl stands as a valuable and versatile platform. It empowers researchers to conduct sophisticated simulations, contributing significantly to the advancement of our understanding of cellular behavior within the intricate three-dimensional landscapes of living tissues.
